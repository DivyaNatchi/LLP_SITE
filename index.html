    A Note from Our Founder

    Karthikeyan Natesan
    Founder & Executive Director

    Over my 40+ years of journey, including nearly two decades in the software industry, I have come to profoundly realize just how much I have received from the world and society. Countless individuals have guided, supported, and helped me overcome challenges in both my professional and personal life. The success I enjoy today is largely due to the unwavering support from society. This realization led me to a critical question that has been on my mind for the past decade: What have I given back to this society?

    This question has driven me to seek meaningful ways to contribute. My career began at Acme Technologies Private Limited, where I learned that one doesn't need to be in public service, defense, or politics to make a difference. Social service can be integrated into any profession. I took my training responsibilities seriously, believing that by effectively grooming young talent, I could lay a strong foundation for their careers, turning them into skilled engineers who would benefit society.

    For the past eight years, my family and I have supported the educational needs of two special children. While this was a step in the right direction, I felt it was not enough. Despite not being affluent, I have a strong desire to contribute more significantly to society.

    Throughout my life, my survival and growth have been supported by society, directly or indirectly. By putting in effort, I have received much in return. With this trust in society's role in my survival and growth, I decided to develop simple software solutions to address the problems or needs of the common public and entrepreneurs, thereby easing daily life. Additionally, I am committed to grooming young talents by sharing my knowledge and experience.

    To fund these social service activities, our organization undertakes software development, proof of concept (PoC) projects, maintenance, implementation, technical support, process re-engineering, and consultation services. We target small and medium enterprises (SMEs), offering our expertise at affordable costs. This approach allows us to support our mission while delivering high-quality solutions to our clients.

    As a responsible engineer, I believe it is my duty to address public needs by providing appropriate engineering solutions. I invite you to join us in our mission to contribute to the growth and betterment of society. Together, we can make a significant impact and create a more inclusive, technologically advanced, and prosperous future for all.

    Thank you!
